Notice


On declaring [MainCompanyName] to be a Recognised Non-AIFC Clearing House

  1. The Astana Financial Services Authority (the “AFSA”) made a recognition order [AuthOrderCounter] dated [*CURRENTDATE] (the “Order”) declaring [MainCompanyName] Ltd, [MainCompanyFullAddress], incorporated and existing under the laws of [MainCompanyPlaceOfIncorporation] and regulated by the [PsRegulatorName], to be a Recognised Non-AIFC Clearing House.

  2. The Order was made pursuant to the section 89(1) of the AIFC Financial Services Framework Regulations No.18 of 2017 and rule 1.1.3 of the AIFC Recognition Rules No. FR0004 of 2017.

  3. The Order comes into force from the date of signing, which is 20/06/2019.

  4. As a Recognised Non-AIFC Clearing House, [MainCompanyName] is subject to supervision under Chapter 1.2 of the AIFC Recognition Rules No. FR0004 of 2017.

  5. Pursuant to the decision of the AFSA Board of Directors on 28 April 2018, the application fee payable in respect of a Recognised Non-AIFC Clearing House is waived.

  6. In due course [MainCompanyName] will appear on the Public Register in the official AFSA website www.afsa.kz as a Recognised Non-AIFC Clearing House.
