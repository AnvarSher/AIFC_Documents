On declaring [MainCompanyName] to be a Recognised Non-AIFC Member

The Astana Financial Services Authority (the “AFSA”) made a recognition order [AuthOrderCounter] dated [*CURRENTDATE] (the “Order”) declaring [MainCompanyName], established in accordance with the law of the [MainCompanyPlaceOfIncorporation], with the Licence No. [MainCompanyRegistrationNumber] dated [MainCompanyRegistrationDate*DATE] issued by the [PsRegulatorName], to be a Recognised Non-AIFC Member.

  3. The Order comes into force from the date of signing, which is [*CURRENTDATE], and is subject to the admission of [MainCompanyName] as a Member of the Astana International Exchange (the “AIX”) within 180 days from the date of signing the Order.
